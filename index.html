Introducing Green Applelec LED Light Sheet,

the design industry's first eco-friendly light panel

Supplying the design industry with the first, fully environmentally friendly light panel, we’re delighted to unveil our new, Green Applelec LED Light Sheet.

 

We’ve been working hard behind the scenes to put in place new initiatives that will reduce our impact on the environment, and after running Life Cycle Analysis on our backlighting panel, we identified areas where we could improve upon our carbon footprint.


From this, we’re excited to announce our new Green Applelec LED Light Sheet is the first light panel to utilise recycled and recyclable materials, creating a more sustainable product.

INTRODUCING GREEN APPLELEC LED LIGHT SHEET

An environmentally friendly option, Green Applelec LED Light Sheet is engineered using Green Cast 100% recycled acrylic, which requires less water and carbon dioxide to manufacture. A kinder option for the environment, our eco-friendly light panel significantly reduces its contribution to air acidification and eutrophication.


As with our other light panel option, Green Applelec LED Light Sheet is engineered at our West Yorkshire premises, guaranteeing the same high-quality solution designers expect and trust from our backlighting products.


Green Applelec LED Light Sheet carries an advanced 5-year warranty.
